Tour Plan

01
Day 01: Chennai

On your arrival at Chennai Airport, our tour executive will greet and assist you. Check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit the Parthasarathy Temple, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and Ashtalakshmi Temple. Overnight stay in Chennai.

02
Day 02: Chennai to Tirupathi via Thiruthani

After breakfast, proceed towards Tirupati. En route, visit the Tiruttani Murugan Temple, one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit the Lord Balaji Temple, the richest temple in the world. Overnight stay at Tiruttani.

03
Day 03: Tirupathi to Tiruvannamalai via Vellore

This day, proceed towards Tiruvannamalai. En route, visit the Vellore Golden Temple, one of the most prominent spiritual centers in Tamil Nadu. Overnight stay in Tiruvannamalai.

04
Day 04: Tiruvannamalai to Chidambaram

Start your day by visiting the Lord Arunachaleshwarar Temple in Tiruvannamalai, one of the five elemental temples (representing fire). Then, proceed to Chidambaram, check in at the hotel, and relax. In the afternoon, visit the Lord Nataraja Temple (representing the sky). Overnight stay in Chidambaram.

05
Day 05: Chidambaram to Kumbakonam

After breakfast, proceed towards Kumbakonam. En route, visit several Navagraha temples including Vaitheeswarankoil, Thiruvenkadu, Keezha Perumpallam, and Thirunallar. After darshan, check in at the hotel in Kumbakonam and overnight stay at Kumbakonam.

06
Day 06: Kumbakonam to Rameshwaram via Tanjore

Early in the morning, visit Swamimalai Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan) and proceed towards Rameswaram, one of the holy places of Hindus. En route, visit Brihadishvara Temple (the Big Temple) at Tanjore. Overnight stay at Rameswaram.

07
Day 07: Rameshwaram to Madurai

In the morning, visit the Lord Ramanathaswamy Temple and proceed towards Madurai. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit the Meenakshi Temple and Mariamman Temple. Overnight stay in Madurai.

08
Day 08: Madurai

This day, explore Madurai with visits to the Alagar Temple (dedicated to Lord Vishnu), Pazhamudircholai Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan), and Thiruparankundram Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan).

09
Day 09: Madurai to Trichy via Palani

Early in the morning, proceed towards Trichy. En route, visit the Palani Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan). Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit the Srirangam Temple and Jambukeswarar Temple (representing water). Overnight stay in Trichy.

10
Day 10: Trichy to Chennai

Proceed towards Chennai. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. If time permits, enjoy shopping in the T. Nagar area. Overnight stay in Chennai.

11
Day 11: Departure from Chennai

After breakfast, our representatives will assist with your check-out and transfer you to the Chennai Airport for your onward journey, carrying sweet memories of your spiritual journey through Tamil Nadu.
